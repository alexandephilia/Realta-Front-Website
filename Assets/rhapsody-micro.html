Rhapsody Features

Front Office Solutions

Rhapsody's Front Office solutions empower staff to deliver exceptional personal service. By automating administrative tasks, it allows front desk officers to focus on creating memorable guest experiences.

Our system provides powerful tools for building long-term customer relationships and identifying revenue opportunities. It analyzes guest behavior and preferences, enabling tailored services and targeted marketing campaigns.

Rhapsody offers real-time integration between Point of Sale and Front Office systems, enabling features like automatic charge restrictions for cash-basis guests and instant updates to sales analysis reports.

For banquet management, Rhapsody streamlines interdepartmental communication and event preparation. It generates Banquet Event Orders instantly and incorporates them into a Tracing System for efficient coordination and timely execution.

Back Office Management

Rhapsody Back Office is a powerful tool that seamlessly transfers operational information into financial modules. It integrates analytical reports across Income Audit, Accounts Receivable, Accounts Payable, Cash & Bank, Fixed Assets, and General Ledger.

Our Multi Budget utility empowers management teams to compare initial budgets with revised budgets and forecasts. Rhapsody streamlines Income Audit by automating sales summary and journal preparation, generating Consolidated Daily Revenue Reports and Sales Analysis by Department.

For supplier management, Rhapsody offers AP Aging Analysis and Payment Schedules with approval hierarchies, directly linked to the Cash & Bank module for immediate payment processing. The system also provides Bank Reconciliation and Cash/Bank Daily Activity Reports for efficient cash flow management.

Logistic System

Rhapsody's purchasing system categorizes buying based on frequency. The Daily Market List handles perishables like vegetables and meats, instantly processing kitchen staff requests. Regular Order manages periodic purchases such as toiletries and stationery. Purchase History stores records, enabling analysis of purchase frequency and supplier price comparisons.

Rhapsody equips Receiving departments with tools for optimal handling of receipts, redistribution, and reporting. The Expected Delivery Report aids in storage planning and proactively notifies of delays. Receiving Record Forms are auto-generated and integrated into Daily Receiving Report Summaries. This module's integration with Accounts Payable ensures simultaneous database updates.

For efficient supply management, Rhapsody provides Department Usage Report and Daily Flash Report to department heads and Cost Control. These tools assist in forecasting and managing supply usage, helping to prevent departmental overspending.
