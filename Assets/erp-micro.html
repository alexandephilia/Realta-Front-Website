Infor CloudSuite

ERP for Discrete Manufacture

Infor CloudSuite is a versatile powerful enterprise resource planning (ERP) solution designed specifically for discrete manufacturers. It's fully integrated and equipped with the tools to improve operational performance across your plant, supply chain, and aftermarket service operations.

This comprehensive solution covers all aspects of manufacturing, including production, equipment, high-tech, and electronics. It's strategically and efficiently built to handle the complex manufacturing processes commonly found in modern manufacturing, from engineer-to-order to repetitive manufacturing.

With industry-specific functionality that stretches from planning to managing the complex intricacies of repetitive manufacturing, Infor CloudSuite empowers you to streamline operations, reduce costs, and design to delivery and beyond. By enabling you to quickly and easily visualize your business processes in real-time, it can change how you work and help you make more informed decisions to grow your business.
